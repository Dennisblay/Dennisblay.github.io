McHan Organics

What We Do

McHan Organics, a Ghanaian-based sustainable cosmetics company, pioneers circular economy solutions by transforming discarded cocoa husks into high-quality cosmetic ingredients. Our innovative approach addresses the agrowaste crisis in cocoa farming communities, minimizing environmental impact and aligning with Sustainable Development Goal 12.

Beyond waste reduction, we empower women through skill development, advancing SDG 5 on Gender Equality. Our natural cosmetic products, including skincare and soaps, utilize these ethically sourced ingredients, promoting responsible consumption.

McHan Organics stands at the intersection of environmental stewardship, women's empowerment, and sustainable beauty, contributing to a greener future.

About McHan Organics

McHan Organics, a Ghanaian-based sustainable cosmetics company, pioneers circular economy solutions by transforming discarded cocoa husks into high-quality cosmetic ingredients. Our innovative approach addresses the agrowaste crisis in cocoa farming communities, minimizing environmental impact and aligning with Sustainable Development Goal 12. Beyond waste reduction, we empower women through skill development, advancing SDG 5 on Gender Equality. Our natural cosmetic products, including skincare and soaps, utilize these ethically sourced ingredients, promoting responsible consumption. McHan Organics stands at the intersection of environmental stewardship, women's empowerment, and sustainable beauty, contributing to a greener future.

McHan Organics has directly impacted 26 lives and indirectly influenced 52 lives through our sustainable waste management initiative, partnering with four smallholder farmers to increase their income, empowering 15 women through skill development, creating jobs for five individuals, successfully developing and selling 1005 pieces of our innovative cosmetic products, and garnering recognition from the Vice Chancellor of KNUST as the most innovative project of 2023,AWARD RECEIVED FROM MASTERCARD as the most innovative community project for the year 2022, won the first runner up for the think it fund it KNUST pitch competition ,made it to the TOP FIVE FOR IAU REGIONAL DEMO PITCH and became the WINNERS FOR IAU NATIONAL DEMO PITCH.

McHan Organics has made significant progress and had a positive impact in a short time. They have addressed the issue of cocoa husk waste by transforming it into valuable ingredients for their cosmetics. They have also empowered women through skill development programs and job opportunities. Their eco-friendly production practices have reduced greenhouse emissions and they have bolstered local economies through partnerships with cocoa farming cooperatives.
