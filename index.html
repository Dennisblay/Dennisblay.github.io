Our Core Experts

Meet the team

DeepMetrics Inc. is a company founded by three colleagues that have known each other for 19 years. After our first degree in Geomatics Engineering, we pursued masters and PhDs in Geomatics and Civil Engineering.

Our core expertise include the application of database systems, geographic information systems, computer vision, machine learning towards improving transportation systems management and operations.

OUR TEAM

Yaw Adu-Gyamfi, PhD

Co-Founder & President

Dr. Adu-Gyamfi is an assistant professor at the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Missouri. His research focus areas are traffic operations, intelligent transportation systems, civil infrastructure systems, Big Data analytics and artificial intelligence.

His research has been supported by various agencies, including the NSF, U.S. DOT, state DOTs and municipalities.

Sampson Asare, PhD.

Co-Founder & CEO

Dr. Asare has many years of experience in cutting-edge ITS and surface-transportation research and deployments. He has served in technical advisory and management roles in numerous flagship projects sponsored by the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) and Intelligent Transportation Systems Joint Program Office (ITS JPO)

His areas of expertise include Connected & Autonomous Vehicles (CAVs) research, microscopic traffic simulations, transportation system management and operations (TSMO).

Titus Tienaah, PhD.

Co-Founder & CTO

Dr. Tienaah is a lecturer at the Geomatic Engineering Department, Faculty of Civil and Geo-Engineering at KNUST. His research focus include geographic information systems, geospatial databases, remote sensing, and computer vision.

He has worked as a geospatial engineer at Resson and through the University of Calgary, provided professional services for Canada Centre for Mapping and Earth Observation (CCMEO).
